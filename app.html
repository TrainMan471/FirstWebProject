What are the origins of the Rosary?

There are several stories of how the Rosary originated. There is evidence of it appearing even in Pre-Christian times, in which repeated prayers and beads were used to help someone meditate. There is even evidence from the Middle Ages that these strings of prayer beads were used to say Our Fathers and Hail Marys. The strings of beads used to say these prayers became known as "Paternoser", which means "Our Father." Between the 12th and 15th centuries, the structure of the rosary evolved and now 50 "Hail Marys" were recited along with the Our Fathers.
